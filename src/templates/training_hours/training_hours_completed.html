Facilities that need to complete the “Executive Briefing Training Requirement” will see one of the two options below:

1. “0 hours completed” -- Facilities that see this response either have not completed the Executive Briefing Approval form to ensure that the correct Leaders have been designated to complete the training OR none of the designated leaders have completed the training.
           If you have yet to designate the leaders that are completing this training, please complete this form as soon as possible: https://hrc.wufoo.com/forms/hei-executive-briefing-participants/

2. “HAPON hours completed” (or any combination of “HAPON”) -- Depending on the letters shown indicates which of your senior leaders have completed the training.

H = Human Resources
A = Admitting/Registration
P = Patient Relations
O = Organizational Leadership
N = Nursing Management

For example, if your listing says, “HAP hours completed”, this means that your Human Resources, Admitting/Registration, and Patient Relations executives have completed the training. We do NOT see a training completion for Organizational Leadership (C-Suite) or Nursing.
